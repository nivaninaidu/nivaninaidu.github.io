Photoshop: We used the app Adobe Photoshop to complete the task. We got given nine photos with some errors on them and had to fix them using different tools.

DVD Cover: For this task, we also used the app Adobe Photoshop. We had to produce a single piece of digital graphic artwork that included a front cover, back cover and spine. The theme for the DVD cover was "Energy Matters."

Menu: Again for this task, we also used the app Adobe Photoshop. For this task we had to redesign the presentation of the menu that was given to us.

Audio: For this task, we used the app Audacity. We were given a few sounds and had to mix, split and duplicate them to create a new sound.

Video: For this task, I used the app iMovie since it was already installed on my laptop. We were given the link to a website which contained copy free videos. We had to choose three videos and one sound and mix them together to make one video.
