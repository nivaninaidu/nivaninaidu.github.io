Video Editing


Our task was to download 3 videos of our choice from the freestock website. We also had to pick a piece of audio to use with our videos, which we found from the freemusicpublicdomain website. The goal for this task is to have 3 videos, 1 piece of music, and to mix them together into something that is enjoyable to view using Movie Maker or iMovie.
